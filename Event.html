Story Of Achievements


Let us take you through the journey to excellence of Sheikh Khalifa Bin Zayed Arab Pakistan School. We have gotten this far through our determination, hard work, dedication and commitment to excel. Our journey is not over yet, still there are milestones to achieve, which we believe we can, by working together.

Renovations At A Glance


Sheikh Khalifa Bin Zayed Arab Pakistan School has adopted almost a new look since the last two years. The management has taken stringent steps for the development of the school. In this regard there has been special focus on renovation of student facilities which hold utmost importance for student’s physical and mental development. From sports courts to green grounds, the dedication and commitment of those working for their development and renovation can be seen.

The Improved Sports Facilities


SKBZAPS has initiated sports activities by renovating its facilities and appointing an internationally recognized sports coach on campus. We now offer a wide variety of sports with excellent coaching and playing facilities. The aim of our Sports Society is to engage all students in some form of physical activity. Being part of a team has its obvious benefits in promoting healthy relationships and well being, but leadership, self-respect, confidence, and teamwork are all indispensable parts of the general sporting education.

SKBZAPS Love For Green


Green is the color of life and a symbol of energy. Green plants are very important for our environment. At Sheikh Khalifa Bin Zayed Arab Pakistan School, we teach our students that green forms the basis for sustainability and long-term health of environmental systems. We make them realize the importance of Green, as plants remove carbon dioxide from the atmosphere and generate the oxygen required for well being and health of humans. SKBZAPS has launched a “move to green”, which started with inauguration of Botanical Garden, in which the students took part with enthusiasm. The dedicated workers and gardeners further extended the work to revive the barren grounds, which have now been converted to beautiful green grounds. The Bird House Garden had been set up as an initiative to teach our students “Care for nature”. We proudly cherish the new green look of SKBZAPS.
